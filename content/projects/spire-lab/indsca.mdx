### Overview

**Indian Speech Communication Association** or IndSCA is a community/committee formed with distinguished faculty members from different institutions of India.
With boosting speech, language and audio research activity in India as one of the major objectives.

This web-page is the **one-stop guide** to the services offered by IndSCA such as upcoming workshops, lectures by the member professors, application of grants,
upcoming and on-going workshops and many more along with possible internship opportunities for current students from across the country under the member professors.

#### Tasks:

-   Developed an **invite-based networking** platform for speech scientists and students to connect through IndSCA
-   Designed and implemented fully responsive **front-end UI** using React and Material UI components
-   Built **RESTful API** backend using Node.js and Express to handle user authentication and data storage
-   Integrated Firebase **firestore database** to store user profiles and forum posts
-   Implemented **access control** allowing admin users to invite new members and moderate content

#### Tech Stack:

React, MaterialUI, Node.js, Express, Firebase, Affinity Designer
